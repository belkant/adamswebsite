A cheeseburger burger is a burger with cheese and burger - yes, plural!

What makes up a cheeseburger burger?

Meat patties, typically bovine, with a slice of cheese, tomato, lettuce and onion.

Cheeseburger burgers are a staple of North American cuisine.

Cheeseburgers can be either healthy or extremely unhealthy.
